A creamy, cheesy classic Italian pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt & black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in salted water.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs, cheese, pepper.
  4. Toss cooked pasta with pancetta, then add egg mixture off heat.
  5. Serve hot with extra cheese!
